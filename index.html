Sara Mahdizadeh Shahri

I am a first Ph.D. student in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department at the University of Michigan.
My Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Baris Kasikci.
My research interests broadly are on bridging the gap between hardware and software stack. Before starting my Ph.D. at the University of Michigan, I was a graduate student at Penn State University, where I got my M.Sc. under the supervision of Dr. Aasheesh Kolli with a focus on computer architecture and persistent memories. I also worked as a Software Engineer Intern at Google in summer 2021.
